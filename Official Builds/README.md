# 📦Explaining the flavours
## Stable
Choose Optimization Pack – Stable for a reliable, well-tested, and highly compatible experience.
This edition prioritizes stability, performance, and broad mod compatibility, making it ideal for long-term gameplay and general use.

Performance may vary depending on your hardware and operating system.

## Rolling Release/Unstable
Choose Optimization Pack – Rolling Release (Unstable) if you want early access to the latest features, updates, and optimization improvements before they are included in the Stable edition.

This edition is tested primarily for critical issues (such as crashes) but may contain bugs or instability.
It is recommended for testing, benchmarking, and advanced users, rather than long-term worlds.

## Vulkan Edition
Choose Optimization Pack – Vulkan Edition for maximum performance and reduced CPU overhead through the use of a Vulkan-based rendering pipeline.

This edition may deliver significantly improved performance on supported hardware, but sacrifices compatibility with many mods, as Vulkan currently has more limited ecosystem support compared to the standard Sodium-based renderer.

Due to its minimal and focused mod stack, this edition is often the most stable in terms of internal consistency.

## 1.12.2-LTS
Choose Optimization Pack – 1.12.2 LTS (Long-Term Support) for an optimized and modernized experience on Minecraft 1.12.2.

This edition includes:

- performance improvements and bug fixes tailored for 1.12.2
- support for modern Java versions via Cleanroom
- improved compatibility with newer hardware and systems

Stability and performance may vary depending on your hardware and operating system.
